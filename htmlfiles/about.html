Why WE Do, What We Do

Dr. Angad Tiwari is a pioneering researcher in the field of gut health and microbiome studies. With over two decades of experience, he has contributed significantly to our understanding of the gut-brain axis and its impact on human health.

His groundbreaking research has led to numerous breakthroughs in understanding the relationship between gut bacteria and various health conditions. Dr. Tiwari's work spans multiple disciplines, combining molecular biology, neuroscience, and clinical research.

Currently leading the Gut Health Research Initiative, he continues to push the boundaries of our understanding while mentoring the next generation of researchers in this vital field.
