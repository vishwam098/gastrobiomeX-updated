Understanding IBD

Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) represents a complex group of chronic inflammatory conditions of the gastrointestinal tract, primarily including Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis. Our cutting-edge research focuses on unraveling the intricate microbiome dynamics that contribute to disease progression and developing targeted therapeutic interventions.
